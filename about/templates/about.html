What is PyScholars?

This is an initiative focused on students in the senior high level. Students would be introduced to Python programming in a relaxed and fun environment.

The idea seeks to inspire students to learn the basic understanding of what programming is, and provide them with the basic technical skills to develop themselves.

Why Teach Students Programming

Computer literacy is quite different from learning how to program. Whilst most students may be fairly comfortable with the former, however little can be said about the latter especially in Africa.
If we agree that education ought to be about developing young minds, there is a lot about programming that helps you learn to think and plan in a logical way.

Next, simple steps or rules to solve each of the smaller problems can be designed (algorithms).
While this skill is derived from the IT industry, it is valuable across all aspects of our lives regardless of our career choices.
