What is PyScholars?

The aim of this initiative is focused on students in the senior high level where students would be introduced to Python programming in a relaxed and fun environment.
The idea seeks to inspire students to learn the basic understanding of what programming is, and give them the ability to do some simple (application level) programming where they could build their own games, websites etc as a team or individually.
